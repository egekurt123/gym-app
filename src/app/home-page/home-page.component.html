My Gym Tracker

Introduction

Welcome to My Gym Tracker

Keeping track of exercises, setting goals, and recording gym progress has never been easier.

Exercises

View and manage your exercises by navigating to the 'Exercise List' view. Here, you will see the exercises you have created. You can edit them and view associated entries. Entries include the weights, sets, and reps that you performed in an exercise session.

Use "Add Exercise" page to add a new exercise to your exercise list.

Training Sessions

Here, you can view your exercise sessions, including the exercises you performed and their associated entries. You don't need to worry about manually tracking each exercise session; when you create entries in the exercise list view, the sessions are automatically generated
